About Us

At Defendo, we are dedicated to empowering individuals of all ages and genders through our comprehensive self-defense tutorials. Our mission is to create a safe and supportive environment where everyone can learn essential self-defense techniques tailored to their specific needs. With a structured approach, we combine practical skills with expert guidance to ensure effective learning.

Our platform operates on a freemium model, offering valuable resources for free, while premium content is available for those looking for an in-depth training experience. Whether you are a beginner or looking to refine your skills, Defendo provides the tools and knowledge necessary to boost your confidence and ensure your safety. Join us in our commitment to fostering resilience and empowerment through self-defense education.
